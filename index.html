EXPERIMENT : WHERE YOU LEARN ---- MOBILE APP

Experiment is a app  developed for Students of SE(Second Year Engineering) I.T(Information Technology) Department of Savitribai Phule Pune University , India.

It has a wide data base of M.C.Q's (Multiple Choice Questions) , F.A.Q's(Frequently Asked Questions), and Old Question Papers from  previous Years.

It is very useful for students to study using their smartphone.It is a life saver before Exam it will surely help you.

Following Subjects are covered in the app:-

 1) Fundamentals Of Computer Network.

 2) Computer Graphics.

 3) Programming Analysis Index.

 4) Mathematics III

 5) Data Structures Fundamental Language.
